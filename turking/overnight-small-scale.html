Instructions

The link will take you to a page containing some plots, you are to provide 10 labels and get a code. Do not refresh the page while working!

For each pair of "Before" and "After" plots, we would like you to provide us labels that could serve as commands that change the "Current Plot" to the "New Plot". These labels should be natural to yourself and others.

 

Brief overview of the interface:

  • Current plot and the data source responsible for generating it is on the left
  • A whole range of plots that you can label is on the right, click the Label button to label it. These are to be compared to the Current plot on the right

You may provide multiple labels to the same plot, or label different plots. If you have trouble spotting the difference, just close the window and do another plot.
